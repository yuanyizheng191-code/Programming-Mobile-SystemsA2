Help & User Guide

How to Add Products

Go to Inventory → Fill in product details → Click Save Product.

How to Mark Popular Items

Check "Mark as Popular" when adding or editing a product.

How to Edit Products

Click Edit on any product → Modify information → Save again.

How to Delete Products

Click Delete → Confirm to remove the product from inventory.

How to Search Products

Go to Search → Enter keyword → Press Search to view results.
